# DMarket Fee Structure (2026)

## Standard Fees
*   **Sales Fee:** 7% (Charged from the seller upon successful transaction).
*   **Target (Buy Order) Fee:** 0% (Buyers do not pay fees, fees are on sellers).
*   **Withdrawal Fee:** Depends on method (Crypto ~1-3%, Skin-to-Skin 0%).

## Tiered Discounts (High Volume)
*   **Pro Trader (> $10k/mo):** 5% Sales Fee.
*   **Whale (> $100k/mo):** 3% Sales Fee.

## Strategy Implication
*   **Minimum Spread:** The price gap must be **> 7.53%** to break even.
    *   Formula: `BreakEven = BuyPrice / (1 - 0.07)`
    *   Example: Buy for $100 -> Must sell for > $107.53 to profit.
